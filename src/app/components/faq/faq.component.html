To acquire a professional loan, simply follow these steps: apply for a loan, submit the required documents, and provide collateral if it is mandated for your specific loan type. Once these steps are completed, your loan request will be processed accordingly.

Getting a professional loan can help you set up your clinic or firm if you are a doctor or lawyer. Additionally, you can use the loan to increase your working capital, pay salaries to your workers or even purchase the latest machinery. Finally, as a professional, you can use funds to enhance your skills by learning new courses.

Interest rate for professional loan is 12%.

Minimum Credit score should be 670

A professional loan may or may not require collateral, collateral is required for a loan if the loan amount is greater than double the borrower's salary. This condition is in place to mitigate the risk associated with larger loan amounts and ensure that there is an asset of value that can be used to secure the loan in case of default. Collateral can include assets like real estate, vehicles, or other valuable possessions that can cover the loan amount in the event the borrower cannot repay it.
